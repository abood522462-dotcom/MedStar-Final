About MedStar

MedStar is a graduation project designed to revolutionize the healthcare experience. Our mission is to bridge the gap between patients and specialized medical care through intelligent, user-friendly technology.

This platform was built to ensure you get the right care, right away. We aim to be your complete health ecosystem, from initial consultation to booking appointments with our 30+ specialists.

Our Services

  • AI Symptom Analyzer

    An intelligent system to analyze your symptoms and recommend the correct medical department.

  • Smart Booking System

    Book appointments and prevent double-booking conflicts.

  • Integrated Services

    Connect your appointments with our network of Labs, Radiology, and Therapy centers.

Our Technology

  • Python & Flask

    A fast, scalable, and secure backend built with Flask to handle all logic and requests.

  • SQLAlchemy Database

    A robust database managing users, doctors, departments, and complex bookings.

  • Secure Authentication

    A complete User Login & Registration system using Flask-Login to protect user data.
